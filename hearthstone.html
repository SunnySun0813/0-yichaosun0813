What is Hearthstone?

Hearthstone is a online digital collectible card game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. Originally subtitled Heroes of Warcraft.

In short, this is a 1V1 battle game where each player has to build a deck of 30 cards and control a hero character. Some cards can activate some effects directly, while others can become minions to help the player win the game. The ultimate goal of the game is to defeat the enemy hero. Learn More

About this project

This is a Windows application written with C++ and QT tools that mimics blizzard's hearthstone.

It implements the basic gameplay of Hearthstone. In addition, it contains 60 DIY cards with unique features, and 30 cards for each player. Players do not need to build a deck before the game starts. The user can control both sides of the player. All the cards are designed to match the character's characteristics in World of Warcraft.

This project does not have any commercial purpose, only for personal interest and looking for a job.

Game's Detail: DIY Keywords
Undercover

Directly summon the minion for your opponent. Undercover minions usually have bad effect on it's owner. If your opponent have 7 or more minions, summon it for yourself.

Commander

To attack Commander minions, you must destory all the others non-Commander minions at first. In the other words, you must destory all Taunt minions before attacking non-Taunt minions. In the same way, you must destory all non-Commander minions before attacking Commander minions. Stealth minions are not treated as non-Commander minions.

Chase

If the Chase minions attack and kill an enemy minion, they will gain another chance to attack in this turn.

Move

If a minion has Move effect, trigger Move effect when the minion is going to attack someone.

Deform & Reshape

When you summon or play a card for the minions which have Deform and Reshape effect, the minion is regarded as "Deform" form. If the minion is in "Deform" form and going to attack someone, it will turn into "Reshape" form and cast Reshape's effect. If the minion is in "Reshape" form and going to attack someone, it will turn into "Deform" form and cast Deform's effect.

Threat

When you play the cards for Threat minions, let your opponent choose 1 of 2 effect, both effects are benefit for you. In the other words, Threat can be regarded as let your opponent Choose One.

Super Freeze

Minions with a Super Freeze effect cannot attack, and died as long as take 1 or more damages. Super Freeze ignores Immune and Divine shield.

Smoke

If there is Smoke effect at a minion position, the minion at this position is regarded as Stealth minion.
